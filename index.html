Mr. Darcy's Guessing Game

Description

Dr. Darcy is just a cute dumb dog, but he has a thing for guessing games and numbers. In his little peanut-sized brain, he is thinking of a number. Mr. Darcy likes numbers. And tasty rats.

Instructions

  1. Your task is to guess Mr. Darcy's number
  2. You can make a total of 3 guesses.
  3. After each wrong guess, Mr. Darcy will give you a hint.
  4. If you do not guess his number after 3 tries, Mr. Darcy will mock you.
  5. If you guess his number, Mr. Darcy will give you a big wet sloppy dog kiss!
